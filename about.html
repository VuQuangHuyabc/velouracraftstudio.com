About Veloura Craft Studio

Where Artistry Meets Elegance in Every Handcrafted Piece

Our Story

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Santa Fe, New Mexico, Veloura Craft Studio was born from a passion for creating beautiful, handmade necklaces that tell a story.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every piece of jewelry should be as unique as the person who wears it. What started as a small workshop has grown into a beloved studio known for quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Each necklace in our collection is carefully handcrafted by skilled artisans who combine traditional techniques with contemporary design. We source only the finest materials - from premium beads to high-quality metals - ensuring that every piece not only looks beautiful but also stands the test of time.

At Veloura Craft Studio, we're more than just jewelry makers. We're storytellers, crafting pieces that become part of your life's most precious moments.
