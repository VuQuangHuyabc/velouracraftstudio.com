Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable delivery to your doorstep

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days to ensure timely delivery for our customers. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly extended.

Shipping Options Available

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, with expedited options available at checkout for faster delivery. We offer the following shipping methods:

Standard Shipping

Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
Cost: $9.99
Free on orders over $100

Expedited Shipping

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
Cost: $19.99
Priority handling included

Transparent Shipping Policies

We provide clear shipping policies to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction with every order. All orders include tracking information so you can monitor your package's journey to your doorstep.

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to all addresses within the United States, including Alaska and Hawaii. International shipping is not available at this time, but we're working to expand our shipping capabilities.

Package Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can track your package's progress using the provided tracking number on our carrier's website.

Lost or Damaged Packages

While we take every precaution to ensure your order arrives safely, occasionally packages may be lost or damaged during transit. If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date, and we'll work with you to resolve the issue.

Shipping Delays

We are not responsible for shipping delays caused by weather conditions, natural disasters, or other circumstances beyond our control. In such cases, we'll work with the shipping carrier to get your order delivered as soon as possible.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate and complete. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact us immediately.

Important Notes

  • Orders placed on weekends and holidays will be processed the next business day
  • Signature may be required for high-value orders
  • PO Box addresses are accepted for standard shipping only
  • Military addresses (APO/FPO) are supported

Free standard shipping on all orders over $100! No promo code needed - discount applied automatically at checkout.
